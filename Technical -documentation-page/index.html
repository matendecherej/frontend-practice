Flats

These have to be my least favourite tyoe of shoe. Aside from being just flat, there is very little to work with. The most impressive ones I have come across are those ones that have a heel change, made by a certain american designer.

I have stumbled upon a few examples such as, Jelly, Loafers, Ballerina flats, Moccasins and slip ons. Of all these I find the most comfortable ones are the ballerina flats of which I have around 4 pairs that I habe worn once each.

I acknowledge how relevant and significant they are especially in carrying out day to day activities inthe office and such but I honestly dont plan on getting more.
